About Us

Jolian Corporate Services is a leading manufacturing enterprise based in Ughelli North, Delta State. We specialize in providing comprehensive manufacturing solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of businesses across Nigeria. Our facility at Flat 85 Nepa Estate Ighwre Ovie serves as a hub of innovation and quality production.

With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, we have established ourselves as a reliable partner for companies seeking superior manufacturing services. Our team of skilled professionals combines technical expertise with industry best practices to deliver results that exceed expectations.

We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, adherence to safety standards, and dedication to customer satisfaction.
