About Us

G-Celeb Cakes Juice & Smoothie Co (also known as G-Celeb Cakes Cold-Press Beverages Limited) is Ughelli's premier FoodTech destination for health-conscious Nigerians who refuse to compromise on taste. Founded by an Aquaculture Tech innovator with a passion for wellness, we combine technology with traditional Nigerian flavours to bring you cold-pressed juices, nutrient-packed smoothies, and freshly baked cakes that fuel your body and delight your senses. From our home in Ughelli North, Delta State, we're revolutionizing how Nigerians enjoy healthy beverages – one fresh bottle at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do your cold-pressed juices last?

Our juices are fresh and preservative-free, lasting 3-5 days when refrigerated. We recommend consuming within 72 hours for optimal taste and nutrition.

Do you deliver outside Ughelli?

Yes! We deliver across Ughelli North, Ughelli South, and surrounding Delta State communities. Contact us for delivery availability in your area.

Can I customize my smoothie or juice blend?

Absolutely! We love creating custom blends. Call or WhatsApp us with your preferred fruits and we'll craft something special for you.

Are your products suitable for diabetics?

Our cold-pressed juices contain natural fruit sugars. We offer low-sugar options using vegetables and can prepare sugar-free smoothies on request. Please consult your healthcare provider.

What's your minimum order for bulk/corporate orders?

Minimum 20 bottles for corporate deliveries. We offer attractive discounts for weekly subscriptions and large events.

Do you cater for events?

Yes! We provide juice bars, smoothie stations, and fresh cakes for weddings, birthdays, corporate functions, and other celebrations across Delta State.
